About Demilitarize DC

Demilitarize DC is a project solely dedicated to getting the National Guard out of DC and taking down the 3 miles of fencing that has gone up around the Capitol. Washington DC belongs to the people. Let's take it back.

Made by Materialize

Today, , marks days since the events of January 6th.

It marks days since the Inauguration, when unverified threats of armed unrest shut down DC. These threats, of course, never materialized.

It marks days since the start of Donald Trump's second impeachment trail on February 9th. Again, unsubstantiated threats were used as a pretext to maintain the fencing around the capitol.

It marks days since March 4th, when "online chatter" of dubious veracity was used as a pretext to halt congressional work. Unsurprisingly, nothing happened.

Despite months of no activity, the abominable and hideous fence still remains around the Capitol.

This unnecessary exercise in never-ending threat imagination will cost US taxpayers hundreds of millions of dollars by the summer. I will not surprised if the US government ultimately wastes close to a billion dollars to prop up a state of security theater.

The Capitol belongs to the people. Give it back to us. Get rid of the fencing and get the National Guard out of DC. We don't want them here. If the US military occupations of Iraq and Afghanistan are any indication, the US military will never leave unless legislators like you - legislators beholden to their constituents - make them leave.

Do the right thing and get the military out of DC.
